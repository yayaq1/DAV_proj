Key Findings & Analysis

Regional Impact

Our study reveals that Asia, particularly India, bears the highest burden of TB cases globally. India's numbers significantly outpace other countries, accounting for approximately 27% of global cases.

Treatment Success

Despite high case numbers, many Asian countries have shown remarkable progress in treatment success rates. Countries like China have achieved over 90% treatment success through systematic screening and treatment programs.

African Context

African nations face unique challenges in TB control, with limited healthcare infrastructure being a key factor. However, countries like South Africa have made significant strides through innovative community-based approaches.

Success Stories

Several countries have demonstrated effective TB control strategies:

  • Vietnam: Achieved 90% reduction through early detection
  • Thailand: Implemented successful DOTS strategy
  • Bangladesh: Improved rural healthcare access
